Japan

13 - 23 December 2019

This website is a dedicated memoir entailing my adventure in Japan with my family. Exploring Japan was one of my most memorable experiences as I was able to scower the city of Tokyo alone while immersing myself in the rich Japanese culture. The bustling crowd crossing Shibuya, the serene & cozy lake Yamanakako, the endless merchandise at Gotemba Premium Outlet - all felt like yesterday. My dream, one and only, is to go back and relive those moments. See you soon, for one day I will be back ~ SDS
