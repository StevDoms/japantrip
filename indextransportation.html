Transportation Osaka-Kyoto-Yamanakako-Tokyo

December 13th

Osaka

KANSAI-AIRPORT RAPID SERVICE

  • Kansai Airport Station - Shin Imamiya Station

  • Ticket was bought at the station

  • Last train leaves at 23:32

December 14th

Osaka

Osaka Metro 1 Day Pass

  • Tickets can be bought at any station's ticket machine

  • Only usable for Osaka Metro, and not JR Stations

  • Cheaper compared to buying single ride tickets

December 15th

Osaka - Nara

JR 1 Way Ticket

  • Tickets bought at JR Station

  • Buy at the respective departing stations

  • Cost: 570 Yen

December 15th

Nara - Kyoto

JR 1 Way Ticket

  • Tickets bought at JR Station

  • Buy at the respective departing stations

  • Cost: 720 Yen

December 15-16th

Kyoto

Kyoto 2 Day Pass

  • Bought at Kyoto Station's Bus Ticket Counter (Closes at 19:30)

  • 1 Day Pass also available

  • Do not purchase the subway option

December 17th

Kyoto - Odawara

Via Shinkansen

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased at Kyoto Station's JR Office

  • Book reserved seats

  • Cost: 11,880 Yen

December 17th

Odawara - Lake Yamanakako

Fuji Hakone Pass

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased at Odawara Station's Information Office

  • Optional to buy. Buying individual tickets save about 1000 Yen/Person

December 18th

Gotemba - Tokyo

JR Bus

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased at Gotemba Outlet Information Center

  • Gotemba Premium Outlet - Tokyo Station

December 19-22th

Tokyo

Tokyo Metro 3 Day Pass

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased at these locations

  • Valid for 72 hours after first use

December 22th

Tokyo

Disney Resort Line

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased when travelling to Disneyland/DisneySea

  • Cost: 260 Yen

December 23th

Tokyo

Narita Sky Access

  • No prior reservation required before coming to Japan

  • Purchased at any station's ticket machine

  • Earliest train from Asakusa Station to Narita Airport is at 06.41
